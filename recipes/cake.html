Birthday Cake


Description

This is birthday cake like my grandma used to make for all the neighborhood kids. Since she wouldn't make any for me, this recipe has become a staple in my kitchen now that she's "in a better place".

As an added bonus, this moist and delicious cake tastes good even when it isn't your birthday.


Ingredients

  • Cake rounds
  • Spreadable sugar product (sub icing if available)
  • Trick candles
  • Friends and/or family
  • A birthday (optional)

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Whisk until smooth
  3. Bake for 800 minutes at 10 degrees
  4. Enjoy!
